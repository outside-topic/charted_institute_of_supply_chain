Who We Are

The Chartered Institute of Supply Chain Management LLC is a professional training organization specializing in supply chain, logistics, procurement, and operations management.

Our programs are developed and delivered by industry practitioners with real-world experience, ensuring that learning is relevant, applicable, and results-driven. We work with professionals, managers, and organizations across industries to build capability, strengthen supply chain operations, and support business growth.
