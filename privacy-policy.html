Privacy & Data Protection

Privacy Policy

1. Our Commitment

CISCM is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use our website, attend training programs, or communicate with us.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name and contact details
  • Professional or organizational information
  • Training enrollment and attendance details
  • Payment and billing information
  • Website usage data

3. How We Use Information

  • Manage training registrations and delivery
  • Communicate training updates and information
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and website

4. Information Sharing

CISCM does not sell or trade personal information. Data may be shared with trusted service providers only where necessary for service delivery.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information by contacting CISCM.

Data Protection Policy

1. Data Protection Commitment

CISCM is committed to responsible data handling in line with applicable data protection laws and best practices.

2. Data Processing Principles

We ensure that personal data is:

  • Collected for clear and legitimate purposes
  • Accurate and kept up to date
  • Stored securely
  • Retained only as long as necessary

3. Access Control

Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel and service providers with a legitimate need.

4. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for operational, legal, or contractual purposes and is securely disposed of when no longer required.

5. Data Breach Management

In the event of a data breach, CISCM will take appropriate steps to mitigate risks and notify affected parties where required.
