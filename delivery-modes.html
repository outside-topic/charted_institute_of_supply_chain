Training Delivery Modes

Flexible learning solutions designed to fit your professional life and organizational needs.

  1. Home
  2. Delivery Modes

Shaping the Future of Learning

At CISCM, we understand that every learner and organization is unique. Our diverse delivery modes ensure that our world-class supply chain training is accessible, engaging, and impactful.

Instructor-Led Training

Classroom / Virtual

Our Instructor-Led Training (ILT) programs provide structured, interactive learning guided by experienced supply chain professionals.

Key Features
  • Delivered in physical classrooms or live virtual sessions
  • Interactive discussions and real-world case examples
  • Practical exercises and group activities
  • Direct interaction with trainers and participants
Ideal For

Learners who value guided instruction, peer interaction, and structured learning.

Online Training Programs

Self-Paced

Our online training programs offer flexible, self-paced learning designed for working professionals.

Key Features
  • Accessible anytime and anywhere
  • Structured modules with clear learning objectives
  • Recorded sessions, reading materials, and exercises
  • Suitable for individual learners and distributed teams
Best Suited For

Participants to learn at their own pace while balancing work commitments.

Blended Learning Programs

Hybrid Learning

Blended learning combines the strengths of online learning and instructor-led sessions.

Key Features
  • Online self-study supported by live virtual or classroom sessions
  • Practical assignments and case discussions
  • Continuous trainer support and feedback
Focus

This approach ensures both flexibility and deep learning, making it suitable for comprehensive skill development programs.

Onsite Corporate Training

Organizational Focus

Our onsite corporate training programs are delivered directly at the client’s location or preferred venue.

Key Features
  • Training aligned with the organization’s processes and challenges
  • Role-based and department-specific focus
  • Confidential discussion of real operational issues
  • Immediate relevance and practical impact
Ideal For

Organizations seeking focused team development and immediate application.

Customized Training Solutions

Tailored Excellence

We design customized training solutions tailored to specific organizational needs.

Customization Includes
  • Training needs assessment and skill gap analysis
  • Program design aligned with business objectives
  • Industry-specific content and case studies
  • Flexible duration, format, and delivery methods
Strategic Goal

Customized training ensures that learning directly supports organizational goals and performance improvement.

Need help choosing the right mode?

Our advisors are ready to help you or your organization select the delivery method that best fits your objectives.
