Who We Are

Leading Excellence in Supply Chain Training

The Chartered Institute of Supply Chain Management LLC is a professional training organization specializing in supply chain, logistics, procurement, and operations management.

We focus on practical, job-ready training designed to help individuals and organizations improve performance, efficiency, and decision-making across the supply chain. Our programs are developed and delivered by industry practitioners with real-world experience, ensuring that learning is relevant, applicable, and results-driven.

We work with professionals, managers, and organizations across industries to build capability, strengthen supply chain operations, and support business growth.

Our Vision

To be a trusted training partner that helps organizations and professionals build strong, efficient, and future-ready supply chains.

Our Mission

  • To deliver practical and industry-relevant supply chain training
  • To bridge the gap between theory and real-world application
  • To support organizations in building capable and resilient supply chain teams
  • To enable professionals to enhance skills, confidence, and career readiness
Our Approach

Our Training Philosophy

Our training philosophy is based on learning by doing.

We believe that effective training should:

  • 1
    Be practical, not purely theoretical
  • 2
    Address real workplace challenges
  • 3
    Use case studies, examples, and exercises
  • 4
    Deliver measurable improvement in skills and performance

Our programs emphasize:

Real-world scenarios

Real-world scenarios and problem-solving

Discussion

Interactive learning and discussion.

Tools and frameworks

Tools and frameworks that can be applied immediately at work.

Professional growth

Continuous improvement and professional growth
